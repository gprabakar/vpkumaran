Let Us Know How Corporate Tax Excemption Works

Make sure corporate tax exemptions are available. It means that companies that don’t have any business activities or not making income no need to file corporate tax returns. Companies that have incurred losses and not recorded any transactions in the financial statement are also exempted from filing CIT returns but should apply for a waiver to file it.

Claim Capital Allowances for Eligible Fixed Assets

Your company can instead claim capital allowances for the wear and tear of qualifying fixed assets bought and used in its trade or business. If a company purchase machineries for their trading, they can claim capital allowances for the expense incurred. Writing off low-value assets not exceeding certain limit is one way of calculating capital allowances.

Tips to Prepare the Supporting Documents

IRAS recommends businesses to keep all the required documents or the receipts to claim for the deduction of expenses. They may be something like the bills paid or the invoices given by the suppliers for the goods purchased.

Record Your Daily Transactions digitally

It’s necessary to record all the daily business transactions in software such as Ms Excel or Google sheets digitally. This will make the things so easy during CIT filing in IRAS website. It’s good to retain the records for at least 5 years for future reference.

The business transactions recorded digitally can save your time considerably and it will change the financial statement in to taxable data to key in your myTax portal.

Learn the Benefits and Offerings Provided by IRAS

Keep in mind that IRAS extends the date of e-filing during year ends by 15 days. There won’t be any penalties when you commit errors reasonably during your e-submission. So get the right knowledge from the right resources to avoid such a thing.

Claim Your Tax Deductions

As a business owner, you can claim all your business expenses except some expenses like your vehicle repair, petrol and parking charges, taxi bills even its spent for your business purpose.

The new start-up companies are eligible for tax deductions because the business may generate low revenue during the initial stages.

Implement New Innovations in Your Schemes

The businesses in Singapore can enjoy the new tax incentive scheme called EIS which offers enhanced deductions for their innovative R&D projects and SkillsFuture trainings.

Ensure Weather You Choose the Right Income Tax Return

When you file your ITR, you will find Form C-S, Form C-S (Lite) and Form C in your IRAS myTax Portal. Choose the right one. If your company is eligible for Form C-S, don’t submit your tax calculations.
